How to Use the Image to URL Converter:

  1. Select an image file by clicking the "Choose File" button.
  2. After selecting an image, click the "Convert to URL" button to upload and convert your image.
  3. Once the conversion is complete, the URL of your image will be displayed along with a preview of the image.
  4. To copy the URL to your clipboard, click the "Copy URL" button. The button will change to "Tercopy!" to confirm the URL has been copied.

Note: Make sure to select an image file before clicking the "Convert to URL" button.
